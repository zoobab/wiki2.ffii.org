How to Do It

Send the questionnaire to a candidate and sollicit answers according to the instructions provided on the MEP Questionnaire home page. Enter the answers into the form in the FFII Participation System or send them to your national mailing list or national helpdesk (see How to help for details)

Update: Roughly all candidates got the questionnaire by E-Mail (from feedback_at_ffii.org) on 2004-06-09. You may however need to push them for an answer. See ElectQuestResp0406En for first results, but also use the FFII Participation System to feed the database.
