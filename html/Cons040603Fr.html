2004-06-03 EU Appel Urgent Pour Reverser la Decision du Conseil de 2004-05-18

--> english, deutsch; Appeal, Conseil 2004-05-18, Reversing, Nouvelles

CEA-PME, ObjectWeb, FFII and some members of national parliaments have teamed up to call on EU member state governments to reverse the EU Competiveness Council's decision of 2004-05-18.

It is unusual for the governments to do this, but in the current case it seems clear that there is no real qualified majority behind the decision, and, as EU law advisors of FFII note, there is no legal reason why heads of state should not reverse this decision.
