Reuters and Software Patents

-> [ deutsch ] [ Reuters Pro-Patent Letter | Media & Swpat | Patent News ]

Reuters, like some other news agencies, has done a lousy job in reporting on the European software patent situation. Their reports are poorly researched and often characterised by made-up arguments of both sides of the conflict, where the pro-patent side gets away very well. Reuters has even signed an appeal in favor of software patents in Europe.
