2004-11-27 EU Commission Presents Competitiveness Report

--> [ Universitarian Patent Movement | Commission | Patent News ]

The new EU Commission competitiveness report was presented by Commissioner Günter Verheugen. It measures regional competitiveness by the number of patents granted and by the number of patent administration officers employed by universities.
