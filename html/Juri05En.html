The European Parliament is facing the challenge of reaffirming the effective exclusion of software patentability for which it had voted on 24th September 2003, so as to be able to force the Council and Commission to conduct a discussion on substance. This page contains pointers to documents which may be worth consulting by members of the European Parliament when amending the Council's Uncommon Position.
