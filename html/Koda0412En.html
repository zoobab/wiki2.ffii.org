2004-10-14 SI ICT Industry Association against Software Patents

--> [ Slovenia & Swpat | industry | Patent News ]

The Section of Slovenian software producers KODA.si inside the Slovenian chamber of commerce (GZS) has issued an official public statement against software patents in Europe and against the EU Council's amendments to the European Parliament's directive proposal.
