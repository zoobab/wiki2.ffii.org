Mailinglists

Here is a list of mailinglists to choose from and here is how to use the mailinglist daemon to un-/subscribe and other things.

  • general discussion of swpats on swpat (this list is VERY active at times)
  • news from the FFII on ffii-news (low traffic, only important things)
  • coordination and event related discussion on bxl (should be only brussels but is used for all events)
  • per country: just look for your country in the list (ie. de-parl for germany)
  • per city: some cities have own lists like munich has muenchen-parl (look for your city in the list)

IRC

All FFII-IRC-Channels are on irc.freenode.net

  • #bxl-ffii was meant to coordinate the brussel events but is now used for anything FFII related. More info is to be found in IrcEn and IrcUseEn.
  • #eurolugs coordination and debate related to the EuroLugsEn Project
