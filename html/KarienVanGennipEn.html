Karien van Gennip

  • name: Catharina Elisabeth Godefrida (known as "Karien") van Gennip
    1. official translation
  • "Staatssecretaris" = Underminister; Karien Van Gennip is actually Underminister for Economic Affairs (Dutch: Staatssecretaris van Economische Zaken) (under Laurens Jan Brinkhorst), but in foreign contacts holds the title Minister for Foreign Trade (Dutch: Minister voor Buitenlandse Handel).
