FFII: Tuesday General Assembly will get streamed

[ GA Streaming Projekt | FFII General Assembly ]

The FFII General Assembly (Tue, Nov 29 2005 15:00-18:00) will be listenable online in 2 different bitrates via the following !URLs:

  • http://apunkt.ffii.org:8000/ffiiga-48k.mp3
  • http://apunkt.ffii.org:8000/ffiiga-24k.mp3

The stream !URLs will be only accessible on Tue from 12H00 to 22H00.

Also meet us in our chat channel #bxl-ffii at irc.freenode.net.
