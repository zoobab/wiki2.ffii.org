Preface

The goal of this action is to make politicians aware that the "compromise" reached at the Council session of 18 May is bogus for many reasons. Since they may receive many times the same letter, make sure you add a personal message of at least half a page or so, preferably with your company logo on it.

When you have sent a letter, had telephonic contact, ..., please note this on the follow-up page.

If you have any suggestions for content to be added to this page, please contact jmaebe AT ffii.org
