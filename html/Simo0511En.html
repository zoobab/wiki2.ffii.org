FFII Draft Action Plan 2005-10

-> [ Priorities ]

The SIMO is the main IT fair in Spain and surelly the biggest in visitors and square meters after !CeBIT in Europe. FFII has been invited by Hispalinux to have a information table in the same pabilion where will be Microsoft and the Junta de Extremadura with GNU/Linex (about half a pabilion each). Additionally FFII has been invited to speech in the libre software conference center with an estimated audience of a pair of hundreds persons listening to.
