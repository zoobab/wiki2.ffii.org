2004-09-07 DE New study about economic efficiency of software patents

--> [ Studies | Patent News ]

A new interesting model is presented in the German scientific paper "Wirtschaftsinformatik"

Beimborn, D., Hoppen, N.: A simulative Approach to Determing the Economic Efficiency of software Patents, in: Wirtschaftsinformatik 46(2004), S. 50-60.

Both are researchers from Frankfurt, Institut für Wirtschaftsinformatik. It is a non-partisan approach, which is quite uncommon in this field.

It is a report from ongoing research.
