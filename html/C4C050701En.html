Campaign for Creativity publish the names of their financers

After several complains from Lobby watch and some MEPs, the website of "Campaign for Creativity" has finally displayed the name of their financers. A version archived by archive.org shows that this old page was not mentionning the name of their financers. The date of modification of the about page on the C4C website is prior to 22 mei 2005 01:13:45 - the date of the latest google cache (Google cache of the page). The HTTP last mod is "Last modified: Mon, 16 May 2005 16:47:25 GMT".
