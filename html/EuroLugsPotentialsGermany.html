Germanys LUG contacts except the !LUGs allready mentioned in EuroLugsAnnounceContactEn. Most of the contacts are from http://www.linux-magazin.de/Service/Usergroups There are some german speaking !LUGs from other european countries as well but think that doesnt matter. We can contact them anyway.

How to edit

The best way to edit this page is to make a note like this (CHANGES ARE UNDER WAY ) on this page that it you will edit it now and then copy it into a texteditor that can handle !TABs gracefully and repaste it here after you finished the changes. Dont forget to take out your "editing" comment when you finished!!

Legend

The left column is used to indicate the status of the LUG.

  • (?) no info not yet contacted.
  • (c_CS) invitation/request sent by CS (see list below).
  • (traffic) the LUG has questions related to the euro!LUGs and there is mail traffic going on.
  • (JOINED) the invitation was succesful.
  • ("P" or other) problems while contacting / reason
  • (????) no information could be found (page is broken or 404 etc.)
