Altnet Seeks Patent Royalties from Filesharing Networks

-> [ Patents in Action | Patent News | EP826181 ]

Altnet Inc, an company affiliated with Kazaa, is seeking patent royalties from competitors of Kazaa who use a commmon technique called filename hashing.
