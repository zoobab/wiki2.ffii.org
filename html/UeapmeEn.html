UEAPME (Union Europeenne des Associations des Petites et Moyennes Entreprises, European Union of Associations of Small and Medium Enterprises) assembles SME associations of all EU member states, totalling a number of 11,000 SME members. We try to keep track of statements and activities of UEAPME on patentability and other subjects concerning the freedom to compete on a level playing field in the digital economy.
