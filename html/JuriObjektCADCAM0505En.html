"Computer-Aided" -- What Dictionaries and Search Engines Say

-> [ JURI Amendments | Title & Purpose ]

The term "computer-aided", especially in the combination "computer-aided design computer-aided manufacturing" (CAD/CAM), is an internationally well established concept with equally firmly established terms in the languages of the EU member states. Whenever the Council and other pro-patent players want to persuade the public that they are not making software patentable, they moreover use the term "computer-aided invention", pretending that it is a synonym for "computer-implemented invention"./
