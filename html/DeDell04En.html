DE Technologies vs Dell

--> [ deutsch ] [ Patents in Action | Patent News | Patent Trolls ]

DE Technologies, a patent licensing firm, has sued Dell in the US for infringement of an E-Commerce patent in 2004, granted by the USPTO as US 6,460,020 in 2000. The patent is also pending at the European Patent Office.
