2004-05-28 Wuermeling Party Top Candidate Criticises Council Decision

--> deutsch, Wuermeling 2004-05-19, news

Bernd Posselt, a well-known politician and top candidate of the Christian Social Union for the European Parliament, the party of EP shadow rapporteur Joachim Wuermeling, mentioned "boiling popular sentiment on software patents", expressed in hundreds of letters which he has received, and promised to vote against the Council decision.
