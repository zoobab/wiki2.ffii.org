The Digital Era needs a Paradise Law "In 1256, Bologna adopted the Paradise Law, it abolished slavery. It was the first city in the world to do this. The world needs another Paradise Law now, software patents need to be abolished. And there is no better place to talk about this, than Bologna." From Ante Wessels' Bologna speech.
