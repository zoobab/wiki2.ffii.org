The idea is that these people work with their national group to find the addresses where the Urgent call should be sent to, they can e.g. ask their national xx-parl@ffii.org lists and coordinate activities such as translating, finalizing(e.g. adding a cover page with the addresses of the letter) and (finally) sending the letter:

What national address collecting activists need to do

  • Find the right addresses - together with phone numbers
  • make sure there is a person on the phone number which can handle such kind of letter
  • Try to organize a translation of the letter, but if you can't send in english
  • Take note and report to Lars Noschinski

Please write to Lars Noschinski if you would like to have yourself added to the list as national activists for the Urgent Call
