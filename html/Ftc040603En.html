2004-06-03 E-Bay Executives: Patents are driving up costs at E-Bay

-> [ E-Bay | News ]

BBC cites E-Bay executives as saying about the patent system: "It's driven eBay's costs up and it diverts time and resources from building the world's greatest ecommerce platform. There are dollars spent on lawyers". This quote possibly comes from the FTC hearings of 2003.
